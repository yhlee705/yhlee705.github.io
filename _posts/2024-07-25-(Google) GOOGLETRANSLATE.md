## <b>GOOGLETRANSLATE</b>

* Text를 다른 언어로 번역 ([Link](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/3093331?hl=en-GB&ref_topic=3105411&sjid=2162334432427783619-AP))

```
GOOGLETRANSLATE('Hello world','en','es')

GOOGLETRANSLATE(A2,B2,C2)

GOOGLETRANSLATE(A2)
```
